A delicious looking roll of sushi!

Ingredients!

  • Seaweed Paper
  • Sushi Rice
  • Crab Imitate
  • Cucumber
  • Avacado

Steps!

  1. Lay out the seaweed paper on a bamboo roller
  2. Set the rice evenly on the seawwed paper leaving space on both edges
  3. Place your chopped crab imitate, cucumber, and avacado in the middle of the paper
  4. Lift up the bamboo roller on the side closest to you and roll it over the center
  5. Pull the edge of the bamboo toward you completing the roll
  6. Cut the roll as desired
