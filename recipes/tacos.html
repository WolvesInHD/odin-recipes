Tacos!

Beautiful Fresh Tacos!

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded Mexican Cheese
  • Taco Seasoning Packet
  • Sour Cream
  • Salso
  • Guac

Steps

  1. Heat up the ground beef in a pan on medium heat. Cook until meat is browned.
  2. Drain ground beef in the sink. Prepare small pan to warm the tortillas.
  3. Pour the taco seasoning into the meat with a small amount of water. Mix thoroughly.
  4. After water has been evaporated, warm tortillas to your liking and plate.
  5. Top with sour cream, salsa, guac, and cheese to your liking.
