Tacos

Ingredients

  • One pound lean (at least 80%) ground beef
  • One cup Old El Paso Thick 'n Chunky Salsa
  • Ten Old El Paso taco shells
  • One half head lettuce, shredded
  • One medium tomato, chopped (three-quarters cup)
  • One cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef in ten-inch skillet over medium heat eight-ten minutes, stirring occasionally, until brown; drain
  2. Stir salsa into beef. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly; reduce heat to medium-low. Cook five minutes, stirring occasionally. Pour beef mixture into large serving bowl
  3. Heat taco shells as directed on package. Serve taco shells with beef mixture, lettuce, tomato, and cheese
