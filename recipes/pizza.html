A delicious looking pizza!

Ingredients!

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mixed Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps!

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Spread pizza dough out in a circle
  3. Spread tomato sauces covering the dough
  4. Sprinkle cheese covering the sauce
  5. Place pepperonis on the cheese
  6. Put the pizza in the oven. Cook for 25 minutes
  7. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving
