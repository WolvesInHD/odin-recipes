Pizza

Ingredients

  • Fresh Pizza Dough
  • Teaspoon Yellow Cornmeal
  • Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • Your Choice of Pizza Sauce
  • 40 Pepperoni Slice
  • .5 Cups Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Cups Whole Milk Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough recipe. Place a pizza stone in the oven and preheat the oven to 500 degrees for 30 minutes
  2. Place parchment paper on top of the pizza peel and sprinkl cornmeal over the top. Then move one dough ball to the pizza peel, press the dough out into a 14-in circle, and brush the dough's outer edge with olive oil
  3. Place two paper towels on a microwave-safe plate, evenly spread the pepperoni on the plate, and top with two more paper towels to cook for 30 seconds. use the top paper towels to sop up any extra grease and set aside
  4. Spread the pizza sauce over the pizza dough. Then sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the dough, followed by mozzarella cheese and pepperoni slices
  5. Use the pizza peel to slide the pizza onto the prehated pizza stone. Bake for 8-12 minutes. Let the pizza sit for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
  6. Repeat with the remaining dough and toppings
